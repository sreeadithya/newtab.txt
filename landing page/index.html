yo welcome to newtab.txt - its an ultra simple and lightweight product that i’ve built where you can just write shit down in the new tab. works on basically any browser and without an internet connection.

✦ all your text is stored and stays there even if you close the tab

✦ bold italic and underline functionality

✦ hover to the top left of the screen for the sidebar!

✦ beautiful darkmode with alt + q

✦ quickly blur your screen if someone comes around with ctrl +q

✦ download your text as a .txt file with ctrl + s

✦ change the font to fit your aesthetic

download

for firefox, go to this addons page

for other browsers, follow this

  • download latest version .zip (11.2 KB)

  • go to the extensions page in your browser (chrome://extensions or edge://extensions or opera://extensions) based on your browser

  • turn on developer mode in the top right corner

  • drag and drop that .zip
